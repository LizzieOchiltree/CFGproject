ABOUT THE CAFE

We are Lizzie, Natalie and Jesse. Three friends who bonded over our love of tea, cake and all things kitsch. We combined all three, and Pass the Teapot was born. Over the years, we have grown from making cakes for friends and family in our cramped Bristol kitchen, to running a stall at local markets. Now, we are living our dream, running our very own cafe in Easton. From the beginning, our values have stayed the same. We pride ourselves on our use of high-quality, local ingredients. And we've been told we make a darned good cuppa! Why not pay us a visit to find out for yourself? After all... there's always time for cake.
